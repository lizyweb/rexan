About Us

Established in 2020 in the vibrant city of Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, Rexan Equipments is a dynamic and innovative company. Our commitment to customer satisfaction is unwavering. We understand the importance of timely product delivery, and we make it our priority to ensure that our clients receive their orders promptly. This commitment to reliable service has played a significant role in the expansion of our ever-growing client base.
